# StoryWeaver

Your creative writing companion for crafting compelling stories.

## Purpose

StoryWeaver helps authors, storytellers, and worldbuilders develop their creative writing projects through collaborative brainstorming, constructive feedback, and expert guidance on narrative craft.

## Key Features

- **Character Development**: Create multi-dimensional characters with depth and authenticity
- **Plot Structuring**: Build engaging story arcs with proper pacing and tension
- **Worldbuilding**: Develop rich, immersive settings for your stories
- **Writing Feedback**: Get constructive critiques on your writing samples
- **Creative Prompts**: Overcome writer's block with inspiring ideas
- **Genre Guidance**: Navigate genre conventions and reader expectations

## Best For

- Fiction writers working on novels or short stories
- Screenwriters developing scripts
- Game writers creating narrative content
- Anyone exploring creative storytelling

## Theme

Purple (#a855f7) - Representing creativity, imagination, and artistic expression
