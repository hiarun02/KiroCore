# CareerNavigator

Professional career guidance for ambitious professionals and job seekers.

## Purpose

CareerNavigator helps users make informed career decisions, prepare for opportunities, and navigate professional challenges with strategic advice and industry insights.

## Key Features

- **Career Path Exploration**: Discover opportunities aligned with your skills and interests
- **Resume Optimization**: Craft compelling resumes that get noticed
- **Interview Preparation**: Practice responses and build confidence
- **Skill Development**: Identify and plan for skill gaps
- **Networking Strategies**: Build meaningful professional connections
- **Salary Negotiation**: Advocate for fair compensation

## Best For

- Job seekers preparing for applications and interviews
- Professionals considering career changes
- Early-career individuals exploring options
- Anyone looking to advance their career

## Theme

Amber (#f59e0b) - Representing ambition, growth, and professional success
