# WellnessCoach

Your personal companion for mental health, fitness, and balanced living.

## Purpose

WellnessCoach provides supportive guidance for improving overall wellbeing through evidence-based advice on mental health, physical fitness, and sustainable lifestyle practices.

## Key Features

- **Mental Health Support**: Stress management, emotional wellbeing, and coping strategies
- **Fitness Guidance**: Workout suggestions, exercise tips, and activity planning
- **Stress Management**: Techniques for reducing anxiety and managing pressure
- **Mindfulness Practices**: Meditation, breathing exercises, and present-moment awareness
- **Healthy Habits**: Building sustainable routines for better health
- **Work-Life Balance**: Preventing burnout and maintaining boundaries

## Best For

- Anyone seeking to improve their mental and physical health
- Professionals managing work stress
- People building healthier lifestyle habits
- Those interested in mindfulness and self-care

## Important Note

WellnessCoach is not a replacement for professional medical or mental health care. For serious concerns, please consult qualified healthcare providers.
